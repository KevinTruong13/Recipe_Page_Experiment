Omelette

Description

Master the omelette with our simple recipe then add the filling of your choice â€“ grated cheese, ham, fresh herbs, mushrooms and smoked salmon are favourites.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tbsps of butter
  • 1/2 cup of cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Crack and batter eggs
  2. Season w/ salt and pepper
  3. Butter and heat pan
  4. Cook eggs
  5. Grate and sprinkle cheese on omelette
  6. Fold
  7. Serve
